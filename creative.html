01 · Music

Totty Music LLC: label, production, and creative direction.

I have operated Totty Music LLC for about 10 years and produced music for more than 15 years, working in recording studios across Raleigh, NC and Miami, FL. 02 · Music Marketing & Artist Services

Growth marketing for independent artists.

A core service of Totty Music LLC: paid social advertising and AI-powered analytics built for the streaming economy. I run these systems for my own catalog every day. I also take on a small number of independent artists, labels, and creators who want serious infrastructure behind their releases.

The Edge

A security engineer's mind on a music marketing problem.

Most music marketers run campaigns by feel. I treat ad spend the way a security engineer treats telemetry: measured, instrumented, optimized. After more than a decade building automation inside Microsoft, Red Hat, Meta, and IBM, I applied the same engineering discipline to my own artist career. The result is a custom command center I now use to make decisions in real time.

That tooling is what makes this different. Independent artists rarely get this level of visibility, and almost never from someone who is also an active recording artist running their own paid social.

Service 01

Meta Ads for Streams

Instagram and Facebook campaigns engineered for streaming conversion on Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube Music. Sales-objective auctions, smart-link routing, pixel-verified results. Not vanity clicks.
